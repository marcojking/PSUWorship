Treatment principles
Warm-graded
Pull cool blues toward teal. Lift shadows slightly. Skin reads warm, not orange.
Grain on, always
Add 35mm-style grain at 4–6%. Counters the AI-render look; reads as photographed.
Contained, not bleed
On cream surfaces, photos sit inside cards with 12–16px radius — almost never full-bleed.
Low-light over lit
Worship + studio shots prefer one warm key with deep falloff. Avoid flat-lit event coverage.
